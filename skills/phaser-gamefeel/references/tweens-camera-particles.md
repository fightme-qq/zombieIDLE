# Tweens, Camera, Particles

Use tweens for:

- button press/release;
- item pickup;
- damage feedback;
- reward emphasis;
- menu transitions.

Use camera effects for:

- impact;
- major transitions;
- danger;
- success/failure.

Use particles for:

- collection;
- destruction;
- celebration;
- environmental motion.

Keep effects brief and readable. Too much feedback becomes noise.
