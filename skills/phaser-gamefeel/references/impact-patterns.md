# Impact Patterns

Small action:

- short tween;
- soft sound;
- tiny particle or color flash.

Damage/hit:

- brief sprite flash;
- small camera shake;
- knockback or squash;
- hit sound.

Major reward:

- stronger tween;
- particles;
- pitch/rhythm change;
- short pause before reward reveal.

Do not apply all effects everywhere. Pick a consistent feedback language.
